## ✨ Features

*   **Premium Glassmorphic UI:** Modern and beautiful 3D metallic-glass design for a visually calming experience.
*   **Tactile Haptic Feedback:** Real-time physical click vibrations that mimic the feeling of using a traditional physical Tasbih.
*   **Smart Reminders:** Custom push notifications (such as Friday Durood reminders) triggered during special times and blessed days to encourage consistent habits.
*   **100% Ads-Free:** Zero commercial interruptions or annoying banner ads, ensuring complete focus during Ibadat.
*   **Multi-Tab Layout:** Dedicated sections for your active counter, history logs, and app settings.

---

## 🚀 Tech Stack & Architecture

*   **Framework:** [Flutter](https://flutter.dev) (Dart)
*   **Backend & Notifications:** [Firebase](https://firebase.google.com) (Firebase Cloud Messaging)
*   **State Management:** Provider / Clean Architecture principles
